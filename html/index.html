Save Polar Bears

Your Actions Make a difference

The rapid changes our world is facing are hauntingly obvious in the Arctic.

Polar Bears play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of the ecosystem. By helping to protect the polar bear, we’re helping to make sure the Arctic food chain stays healthy – for the benefit of wildlife and people in and beyond the Arctic.

Millenia of evolution have prepared Arctic species like the polar bear, walrus and narwhal for life on and around the sea ice. Now their habitat is radically shifting in a matter of decades.

Environmental Threats

Climate change has already removed 75 percent of ice volume in the Arctic Sea.

No more oil can be safely burnt.

Growing amount of human activity in the Arctic and climate change might bring about a species invasion in the far north.

A Polar Bear Tracker

With the help of polar bear researcher we will be able to track and monitor Polar Bears in the Arctic

Their postions are beamed from collars on the bears’ necks, via satellite to scientists, and then to this site.

It allows us to get regular updates from polar bears to study their behaviour. We will be able to protect selected groups and provide them support.

To understand how they travel

map a polar bear's range to determine whether individuals travel vast distances or remain strictly within their home range.

To monitor health

measuring its length and weight, taking samples, estimating its age

To give them medical aid
